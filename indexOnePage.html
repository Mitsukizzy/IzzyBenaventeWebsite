About Izzy

Learn More

A developer with passion and initiative.


With a love of building cool things, I excel at learning quickly and executing tasks efficiently. Always looking for new opportunities, I am ambitious and eager to take on new challenges.

Hi, I'm Izzy


Though currently a second year college student, I devote a great deal of my time to working on game projects, participating in hackathons, and practicing web development. I attended high school in Las Vegas where I studied Computer Science for four years and got my first taste of game development and web design. Now, I study Computer Science with a focus in Game Development and Cyber Security at the University of Southern California (USC) in Los Angeles.

I currently work with a fantastic team on a virtual reality game called ElemenTerra. I'm responsible for programming many of the player-specific functions and also for experimenting and developing the game's flight controls using the Razer Hydra and the Oculus Rift. This summer I'm excited (and blessed) to be working at a AAA game studio for the first time, taking my first step into the games industry.

When I'm not at school or at a computer, you can find me at the beach playing volleyball or on the water paddling for the USC Dragon Boat team. Else I'm probably on the hunt for the best boba milk tea or the strongest cup of coffee in the area.
