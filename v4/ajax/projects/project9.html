Using AngularJS, I contributed to the design and development of Pinscreen's 2016 Elections page, which showcased GIFs made using Pinscreen's tech, featuring Trump and Clinton, the two presidential candidates at the time. Focusing on responsive design and implementation, I worked on much of the Elections page to ensure compatibility on all devices and screen sizes. I also prototyped a new page using ReactJS for the creation of user-made mashups GIFs with webcam integration.
