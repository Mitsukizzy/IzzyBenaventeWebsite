Project Description
Often described as the "Reddit for deals," Slickdeals is the top deal sharing community on the internet. I developed for Slickdeal's Price Tracker tool to alert users when a price drops on a tracked item across a variety of stores. Performed maintenance on price scrapers for supported retailers to keep price information updated. I integrated features using PHP, SQL, Javascript, and jQuery while using HTML5 and CSS3 to convert designs into functional web pages.
