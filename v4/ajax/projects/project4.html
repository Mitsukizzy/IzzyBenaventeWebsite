Psychic
Narrative detective game created for FireTV, AppleTV, and mobile devices
Project Description
Psychic is one of six Advanced Game Projects developed at USC for the 2015-2016 school year. It features a psychic detective in a Telltale-style narrative and is being developed for the Amazon Fire TV. Ensured stable builds, programmed new features, and drove engineering decisions for the game.
