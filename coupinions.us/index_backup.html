The Need

USC students want discounts on food and supplies, but getting coupons is usually time-consuming and tedious.

The Mission

Provide a fun and easy way for USC students to get the best coupons around campus.

The How

By downloading the app, students can immediately start answering opinion polls and rack up points toward coupons.


Coupinions Market Potential @ USC


It is estimated that by the end of 2013, close to 80% of university students will have smartphones. Assuming that smartphone distribution is similar to that of the country as a whole, approximately 16,200 students at USC are on the Android platform alone. With additional development for the iOS platform, there are 27,900 potential users just at USC. Coupinions provides schools and local businesses with the tools to quickly reach large tailored audiences.
